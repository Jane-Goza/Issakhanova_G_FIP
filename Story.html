Our story

About us

There were two friends who were arguing about which of them is more talented. The first friend was a good businessman, and the second friend had extraordinary ideas. They decided to create own companies and compare the results after 5 years. The businessman became the head of a beer company, and his friend decided to open a sports cream company, and both of them failed. In the end, they realized that they could complement each other’s shortcomings and created a company.

What makes us original?
